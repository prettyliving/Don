What You Did

You taught me things. Not with speeches— with repetition.

You showed me how engines breathe. How tools matter. How patience fixes more than parts.

You were constant. Even when I wasn’t easy. Especially then.

Don, According to Reality

  • Mechanic
  • Car guy
  • Emotionally unavailable to tears
  • Unavailable to excuses

You showed up. Again.
